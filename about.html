About

Manu Kalia,
Data Scientist

Manu's career as a data scientist, entrepreneur, and finance executive spans 25 years in large public corporations as well as in garage-founded startups. His domain expertise covers optoelectronics, semiconductor IP, renewable energy development, smart-grid technology, and cloud computing.

Manu holds a Bachelor of Engineering Sciences (cum laude) from Dartmouth College, and an MBA from the Amos Tuck School of Business Administration at Dartmouth. He a contributing writer for several Medium publications, including "Better Programing", and is the co-inventor of 8 filed patents pending in the area of user data privacy management using intelligent software agents, leveraging machine learning and AI.
